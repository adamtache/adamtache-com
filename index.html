About Me


Intuition always told me I would end up studying Computer Science in college, and despite entering Duke as an Economics major, well, here I am. I'm very passionate about software design and applying mathematical and engineering methods to create software that impacts the world.

To me, coding is an absolutely beautiful medium full of infinite possibilities; by simply typing words onto a computer screen, I'm able to decompose an intricate problem into smaller parts and fuse them together into a coherent finished product. I'm able to teach a machine to complete a task using mere problem-solving skills, design techniques, and math. What more could I ever ask for?

In my free time, aside from coding and learning, I enjoy photography and filmmaking, Rubik's Cube Speed Solving, playing with Kaili and Zoe, my Black Russian Bear Terrier and Golden Doodle, and engaging in social internet communities such as YouTube, Reddit, Post Aesthetics, and Hackathon Hackers.

My academic interests include Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Augmented and Virtual Reality, and Robotics. I'm passionate about mental health and am currently a developer for a mental health platform at Duke. I'm always up for learning new things and working on new projects.
