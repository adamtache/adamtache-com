I'm currently a sophomore (Class of 2018) studying Computer Science at Duke University, but I grew up in South Florida in Weston where I attended Cypress Bay High School. I'm interested in creating things and applying mathematical and creative engineering methods to innovative technologies.

I have been fascinated by technology since I was young, and when I was 13, I founded an online channel (TechTechManTV) dedicated to reviewing consumer tech products. By publishing 433 videos, I received 20 million unique views and 30,000 subscribers.
